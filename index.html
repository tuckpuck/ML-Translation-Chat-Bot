Translation Chatbot
A friendly translation bot to help you communicate everywhere.
Go ahead, ask me a question:
  • What is hola mundo in english?
  • Hello World
Translation Chatbot uses natural language processing (API.ai) and Google Translate to offer quick translations into over 100 languages. The service needs only some text to translate and the language you want to translate to. Translation bot auto-detects the language of your text and returns a response.
